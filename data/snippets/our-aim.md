<h2 class="cursive h4 m-1">Eclectic learning time</h2>

* Educators can gather and drench themselves in useful, relevant and peaceful ways of teaching and learning.
* Immersion in educational philosophies including Montessori, connecting to real life experiences, with reading seminars, videos, discussions and resources for deeper understanding are provided.

<h2 class="cursive h4 m-1">Family time</h2>

* Families can bond and integrate their inner and outer selves while cultivating a deeper understanding of their spiritual path and purpose of life. 
* When slowing in pace, perspectives change and we leave with higher expectations of ourselves.


<h2 class="cursive h4 m-1">Human making time</h2>

* A need to be away from the family to not only collect their thoughts, create their personality is the stage of adolescence.
* Adolescents can reincarnate themselves to become social new-borns ready to savor and serve the world.
