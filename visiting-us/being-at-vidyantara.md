# Who can and how to book to come?

## Membership

Visitors referred by members of VidyAntara are welcome 
Rs 1500/- plus food @ Rs 500/- per day

Offered **for families** @ Rs 16000/- per year covering 4 trips.

---

## Educational retreats

Groups of teachers for **residential workshops and retreats**:

* With food and stay for 20 persons max @Rs 1500 per day per person including food and stay. Advance booking with payments is necessary to block the dates.

---

## Others

* For groups of school students 9 years to 15 years, max 10 with 2 adults @ Rs 1500 per student or adult per trip.
* One time visitors @ Rs 5000 per trip for a family of upto 4 members.

---

## Booking Procedure

* Send a confirmation email to [%email%](mailto:%email%?subject=%enquiry+regarding+stay%) with name, aadhar card of the members attending.
* Confirm the menu 3 days in advance from our [available list](%url%food/).
* Stay period from Friday evening to Sunday lunch, booked and paid in advance, based on calendar days available.  Food chargeable @ Rs 500 per person per day, for all above 3 years.
